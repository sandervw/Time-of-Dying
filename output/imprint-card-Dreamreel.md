# Source Imprint — Dreamreel (Source-Style.md)

**Features:**
- Sentences alternate short declarative (5-10 words) with dense action-compound runs (20-35 words); no mid-length filler
- Right-branching clauses; action stacked with coordinating conjunctions ("and... and")
- Register: plain, blunt, zero Latinate elevation; no contractions in narration
- Dominant sense: kinesthetic/visual; bodies in motion, contact-precise
- Signature tic: repeated verbal pivot ("he... she... he") with compressed time-jumps between beats

**Do/Don't:**
- DO: short declarative anchors before burst-action runs
- DO: name body-parts and physical contact precisely
- DON'T: interiority between action beats; no lingering on feeling
- DON'T: Latinate vocabulary; no "endeavored", "perceived", "manifested"
