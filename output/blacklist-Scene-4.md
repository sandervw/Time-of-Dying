# Forbidden Patterns — Scene-4

**Simile Bans:**
- No similes whose vehicle is a household item
- No more than one simile per paragraph

**Cadence Bans:**
- No sentences that end on a single-word coda
- No isolated fragment sentences

**Syntactic Bans:**
- No sentences beginning with `And` or `But`

**Imagery Bans:**
- No silence described as a physical weight
- No eyes described as reflective surfaces (mirrors, water, glass)

**Register Bans:**
- No philosophical asides from the narrator

**Scene-specific Bans:**
- No describing the gas as fog, mist, or smoke -- it pours and pools, it does not drift
- No reanimating dead that groan, moan, or shriek -- their mouths work on old orders, not on pain
- No framing Othelmedir's familiarity with the tower as wonder or surprise -- he navigates; he does not discover
- No describing the muscle-wall as "strange" or "horrifying" -- Othelmedir reads it clinically; only Weirmoth may register revulsion
