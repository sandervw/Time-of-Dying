# Forbidden Patterns — Scene-4

**Simile Bans:**
- No similes comparing the dead or decaying to objects
- No `as one [verbs] a [noun]` similes

**Cadence Bans:**
- No sentences shorter than five words
- No triadic sensory catalogues (smell/sound/texture)

**Syntactic Bans:**
- No sentences beginning with `And` or `But`

**Imagery Bans:**
- No description of breath as visible vapor
- No character described by what their hands are doing

**Register Bans:**
- No philosophical asides from the narrator

**Scene-specific Bans:**
- No direct naming of Othelmedir's cabal identity or former alias
- No corpse-murmur rendered as intelligible speech
- No moment where Othelmedir's foreknowledge of the tower is made explicit to Weirmoth
- No gas described as mist, fog, or smoke — it is a liquid pooling phenomenon only
