# Lore Extraction: "The Harridan's Moon"

## Astral Mythology

- `[REINFORCES]` `[EXPLICIT]` **Harridan's Moon appearance** -- Poxy-faced, perfectly round, brilliant yellow; provides strong ambient light over wilderness at night.
  - Existing doc: yellow moon, marks the night of witches.
  - Adds: specific visual description and confirmation of strong ambient illumination over open terrain.

---

## Creatures

- `[NEW]` `[EXPLICIT]` **Witch taxonomy (Harridans, Cursewives)** -- Distinct category: neither human, undead, nor animal, but may wear features of all three. Known forms include old women, great black half-hounds, and dripping grey flesh.

- `[NEW]` `[EXPLICIT]` **Witch magic is not necromancy** -- Witch magic - the Words of Rotten Syllables - is "profane" and "antique," predating or existing outside necromantic tradition. Capable of curing ills beyond mancer spells. 
  - *Note for claude: this should go in magic systems, not creatures*

- `[NEW]` `[EXPLICIT]` **Witch transformation** -- Shifts between old-woman form and a massive beast: shaggy grey hair, tripled or quadrupled mass, hunched posture, elongated forearms, musk of old forest shadow.

- `[NEW]` `[EXPLICIT]` **Witch stasis power** -- Witches can suspend a living victim: eyes open, color restored, breathing, but wholly unresponsive.

- `[NEW]` `[IMPLIED]` **Witch skin-taking** -- Witches can possess or inhabit a living victim's body. Story implies the witch intended to take the Blue Lady's body.

- `[NEW]` `[EXPLICIT]` **Witch curse examples** -- Curses are spoken aloud; known effects: petrification, sleep, skin rot. A completed curse is likely fatal. Interrupting mid-cast is the required counter.

- `[NEW]` `[EXPLICIT]` **Spider Wolves** -- Pack predators with greyblack fur, thin limbs, long low bodies, and bulbous chitinous heads. Attack in duos and trios from low grass at crossed angles. Bite with pincers. Chitinous skulls shatter with a shield-edge blow.

- `[REINFORCES]` `[EXPLICIT]` **Nickel Beetle venom sensation** -- Felt as "tiny slivers of ice, sliding along my arms, slipping under each of my fingers."
  - Existing doc: kills within ten days; rapid pallor, sweating, deterioration; found near Waste Bridge.
  - Adds: subjective venom sensation; confirms beetle is also found on the moor route between Waste Bridge and Middlemoss.

---

## Magic Systems

- `[REINFORCES]` `[EXPLICIT]` **Witch curses as non-necromantic surviving magic** -- Witch magic is explicitly not necromancy and is described as "antique," directly qualifying the setting axiom that necromancy is the only surviving magic.
  - Existing doc: "Necromancy is the only surviving magic. All other magics fell during the extinction event 3000 years ago."
  - Adds: an explicit exception. Witch magic survived alongside necromancy. Recommended qualifier: necromancy is the only magic among men; witch magic persists separately.
