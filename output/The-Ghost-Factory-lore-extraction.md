# Lore Extraction: The Ghost Factory

### Death/Afterlife

- `[NEW]` `[EXPLICIT]` **Dead redwoods house a single soul.** When a redwood dies, its fibrous structure provides one spirit a temporary refuge from vagabond death.

- `[NEW]` `[EXPLICIT]` **Ghost factories are industrialized soul-processing operations.** Facilities capture, sort, and strip ghosts of individuality until they become usable necromantic material. Desire, Motive, and Energy are removed. End product is a saleable foam-like substance.

- `[NEW]` `[EXPLICIT]` **Counterfeit epitaphs function as ghost traps.** Fake Road stones and counterfeit epitaphs are arranged in rings around factory chimneys to lure and capture uninterred souls.

- `[NEW]` `[EXPLICIT]` **Ghost dissolution into a communal ocean.** Fully processed ghosts lose individual consciousness and merge into a collective hissing white mass. This is the "ready for sale" state.

### Creatures

- `[NEW]` `[EXPLICIT]` **Veyers (skull-cavity undead).** Skeleton undead with the skull top removed and a fill-material in the cavity. Three sub-varieties. Can inflict maladies via head-action or scraping finger bones (Amnesia Sweats, Cataracts, Foot Famine, Misoplexy, Siderophobia, The Haunting Cleanness, The Gaze). Heavy damage without killing triggers rampage.

  | Variety      | Skull Content | Attack                          |
  | ------------ | ------------- | ------------------------------- |
  | Pumice Veyer | Pumice stone  | Hurls pumice like sling bullets |
  | Minnow Veyer | Minnows       | Unspecified                     |
  | Sun Veyer    | A yellow sun  | Unspecified                     |

- `[NEW]` `[EXPLICIT]` **The Overseer / Nightlichen (lich).** Manages the Ghost Factory. Physically: a chromatic absinthe skull on a jade tray carried by eight articulated spider legs (steel joints, scapula struts from a once-sacred cow). Death is hidden in a sapphire hen's egg in a coffin located elsewhere. Cannot be killed on-site.

- `[NEW]` `[EXPLICIT]` **Liches store their death externally.** At least two named liches (The Overseer, The Stillbreather) hide their death in a remote vessel. Appears to be a standard lich mechanic.

- `[REINFORCES]` `[EXPLICIT]` **Wood golems as patrol constructs.** Wood is a fifth golem variety (adds to existing: Wax, Glass, Leather, Stitched Tissue), deployed here in automated patrol roles.

### Magic Systems

- `[NEW]` `[EXPLICIT]` **Ghost capture via mancer's censer.** A ghost is drawn to a smoking censer and bottled with the smoke.

- `[NEW]` `[EXPLICIT]` **Shriving as ghost-reduction process.** Named necromantic technique that reduces ghosts, making even powerful ones compact and digestible for necromantic use.

---

### Magic Items

- `[NEW]` `[EXPLICIT]` **Columbine water (ghost containment medium).** Ghosts stored in driftwood buckets of columbine water. The water preserves and contains a ghost's Voice. A ghost can request oblivion by having a living person drip floor dust into their bucket.

- `[NEW]` `[EXPLICIT]` **Wooden Dagger (discernment curio).** Last carving of a blind artist, varnished in albino tears. Shatters easily, but the carrier may discern more in the words of men. Found in the factory's Shriving Fourches.
