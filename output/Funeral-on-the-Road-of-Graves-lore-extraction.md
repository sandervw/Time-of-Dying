## Magic Systems

- `[NEW]` `[EXPLICIT]` **Standard necromancy animates dead animals, not only humans.** Birchbark raises moldering cats en masse, the first explicit example of non-human corpse animation in the setting.

## Magic Items

- `[NEW]` `[EXPLICIT]` **The Sorrowful Father Mask: one of twenty-three alabaster masks granting false identity and physical traits.** This variant grants leather skin and resistance to lesser cuts, but the wearer burns easily in fire. The full set numbers twenty-three.

## Astral Mythology

- `[REINFORCES]` `[EXPLICIT]` **The harridan's moon is a recurring communal ritual occasion with attached moon-crash folk prophecy.** Villagers fear the moon will crash from the heavens and pray through the night to the necromancer-gods. Expands the harridan's moon from a witch-night marker into a cyclical folk-apocalypse vigil. Post-moon-night cheer implies the omen has failed often enough to become tradition.

## World Geography

- `[NEW]` `[EXPLICIT]` **The Lake of Tomatoes is a regional landmark visible for miles.** Its red sheen is visible under the slate-colored sun from hilltop distances. Road-side settlements cluster along its shore.
