# Lore Extraction: The Coffin Rollers

### Magic Items

- `[REINFORCES]` `[EXPLICIT]` **Violet Jelly recipe** -- Composed from glazed fir needles, knuckle dust, and two slivers of moose antler, prepared in or near hot scented wax. Adds recipe components to the existing note: "unguent which melts tissue, but not bones."

- `[NEW]` `[EXPLICIT]` **Jugflower** -- Pressed, pitcher-shaped blossom carried in a leather roll. Disintegrates immediately on air exposure, emitting a piercing ring that deafens nearby targets. Used against enemies who attack with choral screams.

- `[NEW]` `[EXPLICIT]` **Pure Wheat** -- Revitalizing bread (cracker form). Eating one piece immediately eliminates accumulated fatigue and soreness. Scarce; rationed in use.
