# Scene Audit — Scene 8

## Story Fit

- **Cut or shorten dining hall description** — The outline calls for "a mini-arc in a short number of words" across the whole descent; the dining-hall paragraph consumes description budget without advancing conflict.
  > "The long-table lay overturned on its trestles, a desiccated capon still pinned to the boards by an iron skewer; plums lay scattered beneath the gas-layer, shriveled to knucklebones."

- **Fighting lacks back-and-forth mini-arc** — Outline specifies "ups and downs" across the whole descent; the second invader nearly drops Weirmoth but is immediately neutralised without consequence carried forward.
  > "he sat abruptly down in the gas, yet with his eyes still following hatefully the mercenary."
