Then sprang I forth to the table, and against it sprawling; for now was the great Chrypt-Keep of Carcë shaken anew as one shaketh a dice-box, and the lightnings rent the heavens, and the thunder rolled without cessation, and the sound of subterraneal waters stunned the ear of that chamber, and still that charnel laughter pealed above the tumult. And I, the adept of deadspeech, knew that it was now with the King even as it had been with his predecessor in cycles gone by, when the sovereign's strength forsook him and the spirit tare at him and plastered those chamber-walls with his lifeblood. Yet was I mindful, even amid that hideous storm of terror, of the ninety-seventh page whereon the King had shown me the word of dismissal; and I wrenched the tome from his palsied grasp and turned unto the page. Scarce had my eye found the syllable, when a whirlwind of hail and sleet swept into the chamber, and the candles were snuffed out and the tables overset. And in the plunging darkness, beneath the crashing of the thunder, I, pitching headlong, felt claws clasp upon my head and body. Howbeit I cried, in my agony, the word, that was the word TRIPSARECOPSEM, and so fell a-swooning.
