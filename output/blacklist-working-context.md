# Forbidden Patterns Blacklist: working-context (PREDICTIVE)

**Simile bans:**
- No similes comparing the dead or decaying to objects
- No metaphors comparing human action to animal behavior

**Cadence bans:**
- No isolated fragment sentences
- No consecutive sentences of the same length

**Syntactic bans:**
- No colons used to introduce clauses

**Imagery bans:**
- No visor-fogging or breath-in-helm imagery
- No glowbug, lantern, or torchlight throwing shadows imagery

**Register bans:**
- No modern slang (scene is pre-modern)
