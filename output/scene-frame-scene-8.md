# Scene Frame — Scene 8

## Scene Parameters

**Shape:** Dramatic Arc. Setup → escalation → turn. Short sentences, concrete verbs, weapons and terrain foregrounded.

**Senses:** taste · temperature · kinaesthetic

**Rhythms:** long-coordinated · fragmented
Long-coordinated for stretches of sustained combat momentum; fragmented when a blow lands, a name is spoken, or a revelation breaks the flow.

**Color palette:** violets-and-yellows

---

## Source Imprint

1. **Sentence-length distribution:** alternates short declaratives (5-10 words) with longer periodic or right-branching sentences (20-40 words).
2. **Clause stacking:** right-branching and additive.
3. **Register:** plain-to-slightly-archaic; no contractions in narration; occasional formal phrasing ("as if such it were") but no dense Latinate vocabulary.
4. **Dominant sensory channel:** visual, then olfactory.
5. **Signature tics:** matter-of-fact narrator stance even during violence; named spells and named entities given weight by capitalization; dialogue is short, fencing, with no speech tags beyond "said" and "replied."

**Do / Don't:**
- Do: name the action plainly before its effect; let named entities carry their own weight.
- Do: vary sentence length sharply — short after long, never three of the same length in sequence.
- Don't: subordinate-clause stacking or embedded relative clauses in fight beats.
- Don't: interior emotional commentary during action; observation only.
- Don't: decorative adjectives on verbs.

## Speech Rules

### Othelmedir

**Positive mode:** Usually restates the other character's last line before replying (~50% cap)
**Register:** Archaic
**Sentence-length cap:** Minimum 15 words; no cap maximum
**Signature construction:** Usually names the person he is addressing (~50% cap)

**Saturation cap (constant):** signature construction and positive mode each fire on no more than ~50% of the character's lines.

**Banned:** "What does it feel like" type questions; fragment-as-style

---

### Weirmoth

**Positive mode:** Speaks in declaratives about the past (his fabricated origin tales) (~50% cap)
**Register:** Thieves'-cant / criminal-slang
**Sentence-length cap:** 25 words maximum
**Signature construction:** Most lines refer to a body part (~50% cap)

**Saturation cap (constant):** signature construction and positive mode each fire on no more than ~50% of the character's lines.

**Banned:** "What does it feel like" type questions; fragment-as-style

---

## Forbidden Patterns

**Rolled bans:**
- No metaphors built on weather or seasons
- No `X like Y` similes where Y is domestic or food
- No tricolon (rule of three) lists
- No exclusively muted/grey color palette
- No eyes described as reflective surfaces (mirrors, water, glass)
