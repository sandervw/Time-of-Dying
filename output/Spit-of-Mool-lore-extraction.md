# Lore Extraction: "The Spit of Mool"

## Creatures

- `[NEW]` `[EXPLICIT]` **Gearslave Apes** -- Necromantic constructs built from wood, rusted iron, and glowing crystal. Animated by a mancer's will. Capable of speaking Deadspeech. Five named subtypes: Carver, Stonechewer, Yellow, Heavy, Milk. Carver Apes embed crystal buds in walls. Heavy Apes stand twice a man's height. Milk Apes are pale steel, glow white. Yellow Apes are wax-covered. Stonechewer Apes move with enormous scraping sounds.

---

## Prophecy

- `[NEW]` `[EXPLICIT]` **"Headstone Mountain" in the Thin Man Prophecy** -- A Deadspeech tohmb inscription names "Headstone Mountain" as the landmark above the Thin Man's Sickened Cradle. Full inscription: "In the Sickening Cradle; / Under the Headstone Mountain; / The Thin Man breaths behind a silver mask. / His house shall awaken; hail, Sleeper, Dreamer." Adds: a named geographical anchor and the silver mask detail.

- `[REINFORCES]` `[EXPLICIT]` **Prophecy Inscribed in Oldvault Structures** -- The Sickened Cradle prophecy exists in physical written form on marble within grot tohmb structures, carved in Deadspeech. Suggests the prophecy predates or is contemporary with grot mancers.

---

## Otherworld Geography

- `[REINFORCES]` `[EXPLICIT]` **Oldvaults Contain Active Biomes** -- Mool's Chrypt includes a self-sustaining underground ecosystem (Heather Vault): magenta heather, crystal-lattice lighting, mirrored sunlight conduits from the surface, and a populated underground lake. Maintained by gearslave automaton labor under a sleeping mancer's will.

- `[NEW]` `[EXPLICIT]` **Headstone Mountain** -- Named geographical landmark. Located above or around the Thin Man's Sickened Cradle. Source is prophecy inscription only. Literal vs. metaphorical status unconfirmed.

---

## Astral Mythology

- `[REINFORCES]` `[EXPLICIT]` **Moon Described as Humpbacked** -- The moon has a humpbacked silhouette. Adds physical description to the harridan's moon entry. Described here with silver moonlight rather than yellow; whether a separate quality or contextual variant is unresolved.
