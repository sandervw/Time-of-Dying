# Vestibule Before the Demon's Chamber — Action Blobs

## 3. ACTION

`The silver-clad dead peel from the walls in a slow first wave. Engage them before they form ranks; once the choral voice synchronizes across multiple corpses, their movements sharpen and your openings shrink. Target the jaw on each one first. A shattered mandible kills the singing in that body, though the sound reroutes through the overhead pipes rather than dying out. If they surround you, fall back to the vestibule pillars and funnel the dead into single file. Take them apart one at a time and keep moving between strikes.`

## 6. ACTION

`The full congregation rises at once. Cut through them with heavy swings and do not stop to confirm kills. Your blade will catch on the silver cloth; pull it free with a sharp twist rather than yanking straight back, or the fabric wraps the edge and dulls your next cut. Note that severed limbs and split torsos do not go still. They vibrate against the stone floor and hum, adding a low drone to the growing choir. Keep your mouth shut. Every shout, every grunt, every muttered word feeds the song the same as a kill does, and you cannot afford to give the demon both at once.`

## 7. ACTION

`The dead press in from all sides now, singing in a harmony built from your own stolen breath. Shield-bash to create space, follow with pommel strikes to stagger the closest, then sweep low to clear a corridor toward the far door. Do not hold ground. If you stop moving, the bodies stack against you faster than you can cut them down. Every corpse that falls adds another voice to the swell behind you, so carve forward and do not look back. Reach the door.`
