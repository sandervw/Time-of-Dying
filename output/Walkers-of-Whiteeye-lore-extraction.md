# Lore Extraction: The Walkers of Whiteeye

### Theogony

- `[REINFORCES]` `[EXPLICIT]` **Lady Horsehair commands a servitor class called dollmakers.** They wove the Waste from shed animal pelts on her order. Existing doc lists her as "The Girl Doll" with no detail. This story adds: a named servitor class and a documented act of domain-shaping.

- `[REINFORCES]` `[EXPLICIT]` **Lady Horsehair's domain is territorial and possessive.** Her act of walling off the Waste as a "door" aligns with her sin of Greed. Existing doc: God of Possession, details vague. This story adds: the Waste's creation as a concrete mythological act.

---

### Creatures

- `[NEW]` `[EXPLICIT]` **The Hearse.** A two-ton dragonfly-like flying predator. Tail of a hundred stingers. Wings covered in a thousand eyes. Nocturnal. Kills exactly one victim per exposed night. Produces a deep rumbling Stomach Song that rises each sunset and fades at sunrise. Humans can mimic the call to summon it ("Hungry calls to hungry").

- `[NEW]` `[EXPLICIT]` **Stump Dugs.** Dog-like pack predators. Coarse burned-hair texture, matte white eyes, soft wriggling teeth. Their bite carries an incurable Blight.

- `[NEW]` `[EXPLICIT]` **Nickel Beetle.** Small venomous insect found near the Waste Bridge. Venom kills within ten days with no known cure. Symptoms: rapid pallor, sweating, deterioration.

---

### Death / Afterlife

- `[NEW]` `[IMPLIED]` **Proper interment requires actual earth beneath the epitaph.** The Waste Bridge spans open air with no earth below. The narrator considers burial there and calls the idea "unclean," implying interment rules require earth under the marker, not structural suspension.

---

### Astral Mythology

- `[REINFORCES]` `[EXPLICIT]` **The Hearse is constrained by the lunar cycle.** It hunts at night, silhouetted against the moon. Its Stomach Song fades at sunrise and intensifies toward sunset. Existing doc: Gallowspooks are constrained by moonlight presence or absence. This story adds: The Hearse as a second creature with a strict lunar activity boundary.

---

### World Geography

- `[NEW]` `[EXPLICIT]` **Whiteeye Waste.** Lady Horsehair ordered her dollmakers to weave a curtain of dust from shed winter animal pelts, creating the Waste as a barrier between Gallbladder's territory (The Lake of Tomatoes) and the civilized Road. *"A door erected must be, / Between Middlemoss, / And the Red Lake of Gallbladder."*
  - **The Road of Graves is buried within the Waste.** The Road has vanished under black-grey dust. Walkers navigate by astrolabe rather than the Road itself.
  - **The Waste Bridge spans a massive canyon.** A canyon of immense scale cuts through the Waste, crossed by a single unsupported arc of whitish rock. The arc narrows at center to twin stone beams wide enough for one person each.

- `[NEW]` `[EXPLICIT]` **Middlemoss is the geographic center of the Road.** Positioned between two white peaks with high heath between them. Known as "The Heart of an endless road."
