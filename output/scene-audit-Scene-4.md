# Scene Audit — Scene-4

## Story Fit

- **Add Red Pew awakening** — Outline specifies fighting involves both cabals (invaders and tower residents); only Milkmoot invaders appear.
  > "Two more came along the colonnade, then three after them. Old invaders all of them..."

- **Revise hornet-husk attribution** — Outline says Ostrobone the Rook personally wore a hornet-husk mask; the scene implies it was retinue-level token, which softens the lore beat.
  > "He knew the rook whose retinue had carried such tokens"

- **Sharpen withheld treatment of Othelmedir's name** — Outline marks the old cabal-name as fully withheld this scene; "Othelmedir read the shape of it on the way past" edges toward disclosure.
  > "his mouth was working without sound...and Othelmedir read the shape of it on the way past and did not slow his step"

## Internal Quality

- **Fix referent ambiguity** — "it" in "the slope it remembered without permission" is unclear; refers to hip, measure, or slope.
  > "his hip carrying him by some old measure of the slope it remembered without permission"

- **Remove dupe root word** — "darkened in dark bruises" repeats the same root in the same breath.
  > "the silk darkened in dark bruises that stayed bruised after the foot had passed"

- **Resolve thong continuity** — Pendant is pocketed first, but the husk is described as on "the same thong"; if the pendant is gone the thong reference breaks.
  > "The pendant went into his cloak. At the dead man's belt a hornet-husk had been threaded on the same thong as a knife"
