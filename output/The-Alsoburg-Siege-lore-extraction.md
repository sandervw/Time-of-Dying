# Lore Extraction: The Alsoburg Siege

## Creatures

**The Crawling Silk** `[NEW]` `[EXPLICIT]` (Cursed Mancer-Entity)
A former human mancer punished by the Mother of Worms with eternal life fused to eternal madness. Quadrupedal; triple-stranded spine; hoofed elbows and knees; drags a white beard as a carpet. Origin and anatomy detailed in story sections "Abased Ontogeny" and "Backstory." Distinct from golems and undead -- defined by voluntary cursed transformation, not necromantic construction.

**Hides** `[NEW]` `[EXPLICIT]` (Skin-Clad Servants)
Human slaves of The Crawling Silk. Flay victims, wear skins as clothing, sharpen harvested bones into weapons (spine-sewn flails, femur-spike war picks).

**Herdybey** `[NEW]` `[EXPLICIT]` (Cave Creature)
Known to chryptsackers. Two-tone howl ("AHHHH-woooo"). Kept at bay by good blades in cool caves. Mating bulls in humid holes are especially dangerous. Not to be confused with the Leatherwyrm.

**Dream of the Winter Storm** `[NEW]` `[EXPLICIT]` (Crawling Silk Combat Ability)
Pulls a target into an isolated frozen dream-space via "nod of scalp." Golems and Hides cannot follow. Movement is slowed. Kills by freezing the mind within five minutes. White hair-serpents continuously entangle the victim's feet. Document also under Magic Systems. Comparable to but distinct from the Wooly Vampire's Katabasis.

**The Crawling Silk's Hair Cocoon** `[NEW]` `[EXPLICIT]`
When wounded, The Crawling Silk encases itself in hardened white hair at marble density. Cannot be cut. Must be broken by golem-impact.

## Theogony

**Mother of Worms: Active Divine Punishment** `[NEW]` `[EXPLICIT]`
Destroyed six members of a seven-mancer heptad for crossing the threshold of her sacred site (the Chrypt of the Fermented Princess). Spared the seventh, then granted eternal life while simultaneously cursing him to eternal groveling madness. Paired boon-and-curse matches the setting's pseudo-immortality pattern.
- Existing doc: Mother of Worms feeds off consorts; Lust/Decay domain; no documented divine punishment.
- Adds: Territorial divine intervention; establishes the Chrypt of the Fermented Princess as a protected sacred site.

**Chrypt of the Fermented Princess** `[NEW]` `[EXPLICIT]` (Sacred Site)
Named sacred site of the Mother of Worms. Trespass triggers lethal divine retribution.

**The Thin Man's Pseudo-Immortality as Object of Desire** `[REINFORCES]` `[EXPLICIT]`
Secular mancers outside the Thin Man's following actively sought his form of eternal life. Frames the Sickened Cradle as a coveted prize, not only a prophetic framework.
- Existing doc: The Sickened Cradle's opening will unleash a plague for final rest.
- Adds: The Thin Man's immortality (youth + slumber) was independently pursued by outside mancers.

## Prophecy

**The Harmonies of Avda / Harmony of False Seven** `[NEW]` `[EXPLICIT]` (Song Cycle)
A named mythological song cycle encoding creature-lore and historical accounts. "The Harmony of False Seven" is a specific entry. The protagonist identifies The Crawling Silk from it. Implies an in-world encyclopedic oral tradition. No such tradition was previously documented.

**Deadspeech Hymn of The Crawling Silk (Alternate Translation)** `[NEW]` `[EXPLICIT]`
The Crawling Silk and Hides chant a Deadspeech hymn. This story's translation differs from the version in Magic-Systems.md.
- Existing: *"Day of wrath, evil day; shall sky and road in ashes lay"*
- This story: *"Day of wrath, evil sky; shall all the Road in blackness lie / As Seven bid men's souls to die / What horror must o'ercome the heart; when Mancer Silk shall start / And bids the bones by blackened art."*
- Variants ("evil sky" vs. "evil day," "blackness" vs. "ashes") reflect doctrinal or regional transmission drift.

## Magic Systems

**Golem Construction Constraint Clarification** `[REINFORCES]` `[EXPLICIT]`
Golems cannot be molded "under sun or stars." The constraint is celestial exposure, not surface location. Underground construction is viable.
- Existing doc: "Cannot be created under the sky or sun."
- Adds: Stars are an explicit part of the constraint; underground golem construction is confirmed possible.
