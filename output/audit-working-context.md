## CONFIRMED VIOLATION

**Rule:** No consecutive sentences of the same length  
**Location:** Lines 21-33 — the child's numbered rite  
**Offending text:**
> "One. Lamp of the unlit hour."  
> "Two. Quiet for the broken oath."  
> "Three. Bread without the salt."  
> "Four. Door without the name."  
> "Five. Hand without the ring."

Five consecutive sentences in identical syntactic form and near-identical length. Even as ritual speech, the cadence ban applies structurally.
