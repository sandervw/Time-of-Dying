# Lore Extraction: The Temple of Lady Horsehair

### Folklore

- `[NEW]` `[EXPLICIT]` **The Emerald House** -- Lady Horsehair's temples. In Middlemoss: arved into the canyon cliff wall. Operates as an art gallery front for soul-harvesting.
  - *Note to claude: add this and everything below under folklore, in the guilds/organizations docs*

- `[NEW]` `[EXPLICIT]` **Lady Horsehair's temple hierarchy** -- Led by a "Grand Daughter." Staffed by ranked priestesses with distinct violence-talents: Wailing (grief rituals that stop hearts), Tonguebinder (prayers that stymie a mancer's mind), Gargoyle (stone-skinned fighters), Living Hair (close-combat).

- `[NEW]` `[EXPLICIT]` **Tonguebinder as anti-mancer counter** -- Tonguebinder prayers to Lady Horsehair specifically stymie a mancer's mind. Theologically-grounded counter to necromantic spellwork.

- `[NEW]` `[EXPLICIT]` **Lady Horsehair's curse-laying power** -- Priestesses invoke this as a direct threat: interference with a temple rite earns a permanent curse on the spine.

- `[REINFORCES]` `[EXPLICIT]` **Lady Horsehair alias "The Porcelain Girl Doll"** -- Existing doc has "The Girl Doll." This story adds "Porcelain" as a qualifier, suggesting a more specific epithet in the context of soul-doll worship.

- `[NEW]` `[EXPLICIT]` **The Monks of the Lidless** -- Monastic order identifiable by shaved scalp and a tattooed small mouth inside a black ink circle. Present on Middlemoss chasm bridges. Hostile toward Lady Horsehair's supplicants. Not linked to any of the seven necromancer-gods. Name may reference an eye motif. No existing document covers them.

### Magic Items

- `[NEW]` `[EXPLICIT]` **The Unhue** -- Small plain clay jar of acid. Destroys the painted image and severs the phylactery contract. Created by temple chemist Squide. Stored in the temple's chamber of paints.

- `[NEW]` `[EXPLICIT]` **Monkshood Potion** -- Silences armor and footsteps entirely for sixty-three minutes. Concealed in a decorative glass statue in The Emerald House's garden, released by a riddle-lock mechanism.

### Creatures

- `[REINFORCES]` `[EXPLICIT]` **Mimic (portrait-disguise variant)** -- Disguises as a flat portrait on a wall. Attacks by enveloping and crushing the target. Conceals passages behind it. Defeated by repeated blunt-force strikes from a shield boss. Existing doc had "Mimic -- TODO."

- `[REINFORCES]` `[EXPLICIT]` **Wax golems: paint-hardening variant** -- Wax golem surfaces coated in paint harden to stone-hardness in painted areas. Blunt attacks and imprecise cuts are useless against those zones. "Painted Twins" variant: nine feet tall, roughly 1000 lbs, partially painted. Only precise cuts and stabs work.
