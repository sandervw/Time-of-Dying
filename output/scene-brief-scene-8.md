# Scene Scene 8 Brief

## Premise

In Sulky Wend, the living dream the deaths of a cabal sealed inside the Tower of the Attercop. Othelmedir built the trap years ago under another name: his Red Pew released a preserving gas during a Milkmoot attack, sealing all inside as dreaming undead. He returns with the sellsword Weirmoth to destroy the cauldron, but the gas preserved his colleagues, who now wake.

## Scene Structure

- POV: Limited 3rd-person (Othelmedir)
- Tense: Past
- Target wordcount: 1,150

## Scene Outline

(**Action Scene**) Othelmedir and Weirmoth watch as the brewed poison destroys the muscle-wall. They descend toward the cauldron still spewing its slow gas; fighting through several members of both cabals. The identification of Othelmedir and the old name he wore is made clear. The scene ends with the two arriving the Cauldron Chamber.

- **Vicious, excruciatingly-specific fighting** throughout: between the two reanimating cabals (Milkmoot invaders and Red Pew tower residents) and Othelmedir/Weirmoth. Describe specific maneuvers, movements, numbers of foes, how bodies are affected; the fight should have back/forth, ups and downs — capture a mini-arc in a short number of words.
- Dining hall description: food rotten and desiccate, chairs overturned, candles burnt to nubs.
- They pass by a ritual circle where the tower cabal formerly performed necromancies.
- Two named, now-undead former comrades — Fellow Sourbell and Fellow Alder the Lank — reveal Othelmedir's betrayal and identity: 'Fellow Liddick'. Weirmoth helps slay them.

## Carry-Forward State

- Weirmoth just woke from the witness-draught carrying Othelmedir's memory of the betrayal; he has not yet spoken of it.
- Othelmedir has also brewed a second potion (the Lidd Decoction). The muscle-wall poison is the larger brew.
- The muscle-wall (woven flesh barrier over the passage to the lower sanctums) is intact at scene open; the poison destroys it.

- Cabals: tower = **Red Pew**; invaders = **Milkmoot**, led by **Ostrobone the Rook**.
- Weirmoth now knows what Othelmedir did.
- Sourbell's signature: **Cope of Blue Wasps**. Alder the Lank: bass, potent necromancies.
