# Lore Extraction: The Duel

## Cosmogony

- `[NEW]` `[EXPLICIT]` **Lendendrung: golem built to kill the first Demon King.** Necromancers could not use risen dead against Surveillance (his spy network detected all necromancy), so they secretly commissioned smiths to build the copper golem Lendendrung. When celestial omens were propitious, they animated it with a flood of human souls.

- `[NEW]` `[EXPLICIT]` **Surveillance was the first Demon King to die.** His assassination was the opening move of the origin war. His surveillance network had made conventional necromantic tactics impossible.

- `[NEW]` `[EXPLICIT]` **Lendendrung was destroyed killing Surveillance.** The golem expended every animating soul in the battle and was "forever destroyed in turn," establishing that soul-powered constructs burn out their fuel in sufficient-scale combat.

---

## Theogony

- `[NEW]` `[EXPLICIT]` **Surveillance: named Demon King and master of spies.** He was the greatest spymaster among all Demon Kings. He elevated men from slave-lists to serve as crows, ravens, and vultures (informant tiers). His spy reports preempted necromantic plots before formation. This is the first Demon King described by name, role, and pre-death function in any document.

---

## World Geography

- `[NEW]` `[IMPLIED]` **The Meathook Mountains: a named far landmark.** Referenced as a distant travel destination beyond the gorge.

- `[NEW]` `[EXPLICIT]` **The Tunnel of the Stillbreather: a named underground route.** An Engraved Nautilus Oddboon contains a map through this tunnel. The Stillbreather is an existing documented Lich.

---

## Astral Mythology

- `[REINFORCES]` `[EXPLICIT]` **Comets and heavenly signs as timing omens for major ritual acts.** Necromancers waited for "signs in the comets and the heavens" before animating Lendendrung. Celestial observation governs the timing of large-scale necromantic workings. Existing doc covers the slate sun, harridan's moon, and Amber Ox constellation. This adds comets as prophetic or calendrical omens.

---

## Magic Items

- `[NEW]` `[EXPLICIT]` **Engraved Nautilus: ivory map shell.** A polished ivory nautilus shell engraved with a map through the Tunnel of the Stillbreather. Found as a random Oddboon.

- `[NEW]` `[EXPLICIT]` **Infant with a Radiant Scalp: a living Oddboon.** A living infant found in a leather sack beneath a sodden log. The Middlemoss Mayor pays a reward for the child's return. The Radiant Scalp implies supernatural origin; mechanics are unstated.

- `[NEW]` `[EXPLICIT]` **Soul Scarabs: a traded commodity.** Guilda the Scarab Seller stocks a wide diversity of soul scarabs with stock changing every daybreak. Soul scarabs are not currently documented in `Magic-Items.md`. This is a first reference.
