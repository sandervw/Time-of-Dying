# Scene Scene-4 Context

## Premise

Limited 3rd past, Othelmedir POV; Scene 4 wordcount 1,100. Othelmedir, a necromancer, has secretly returned to the Tower of the Attercop, a sealed chrypt whose escaping gas makes Sulky Wend villagers dream the deaths of the dead ("The Dreamreel"). Years ago, under another name, he triggered the gas-release that sealed his own colleagues inside as dreaming undead during a rival cabal's breach. He has hired the sellsword Weirmoth to reach and destroy the cauldron, without disclosing that the preserved corpses include his former cabal-mates, who now wake.
