# Forbidden Patterns — dreamreel-s3 (Predictive)

**Simile bans:**
- No metaphors comparing human action to animal behavior
- No similes whose vehicle is a tool or weapon

**Cadence bans:**
- No sentences that end on a single-word coda
- No `not X, but Y` constructions

**Syntactic bans:**
- No comma splices

**Imagery bans:**
- No visor-fogging or breath-in-helm imagery
- No description of breath as visible vapor

**Register bans:**
- No Latinate vocabulary where Anglo-Saxon would do
