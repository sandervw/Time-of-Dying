# Lore Extraction: The Tower of Silence

## Creatures

- `[NEW]` `[EXPLICIT]` **The Lidless** -- Malevolent winged female half-demons born from two half-souls. Appear as mutated spotted owls with partially human faces and eye-covered feathers. Haunt churches, towers, and barns.

- `[REINFORCES]` `[EXPLICIT]` **Will-O-Wisp** -- Incorporeal. Only necromancy can destroy them. Wisps expend energy quickly and wear down over time. Existing doc covers sound-stealing and brightness; this story adds the incorporeality and necromancy-only destruction rules.

- `[REINFORCES]` `[EXPLICIT]` **Starshadow** -- Active guardian-warden of The Tower of Silence. Enforces internal rules (e.g., curses those who take more than one statue). Existing doc labels it a type of Wraith; this story adds the individual guardian function and named-entity implication.

## Magic Items

- `[NEW]` `[EXPLICIT]` **The Quieting Mirror** -- Relic owned by the Monks of the Lidless. Described as quieting one's thinking. Sought as a cure for night terrors.

- `[NEW]` `[EXPLICIT]` **Glowbug in Amber** -- An insect imbued with un-life by a mancer, then frozen in amber. Glows persistently. First documented example of un-life imbued into a non-humanoid organism as a light-source curio.

- `[NEW]` `[EXPLICIT]` **Scroll of Centipedes** -- Named scroll relic in the Monks of the Lidless reliquary. Function unstated. Low mythological weight; include if the items catalogue is comprehensive.

## Hero Cycles

- `[NEW]` `[EXPLICIT]` **"Strength and Promise" as a knightly creed** -- Appears in the knight-protagonist's internal monologue as a paired virtue-formula, functioning as a chivalric oath structure.

- `[NEW]` `[IMPLIED]` **Horned Morning Stars as dishonorable weapons** -- The protagonist's internal monologue marks the Horned Morning Star as a weapon of men without heart, implying a moral hierarchy among weapon types within the setting's chivalric culture.

## Folklore (Organizations)

- `[REINFORCES]` `[EXPLICIT]` **Monks of the Lidless: full institutional detail** -- Internal structure: outcasts (blind monks) are exiled to The Wine Chrypt, wrapped in owl-feather blankets, fed on soured ancient wine. Tower of Silence is the order's seat in Deep Compost. Will-O-Wisps are bound as guards. Three named fighter types: Tuskhands, Man of Dogs, Leaping Monk. Leadership: the Senseless King and Queen, unliving crowned figures with owl, goat, snake, and spider eye tattoos covering their skulls, wielding iron-hook scepters. Order maintains a sacred reliquary. Order has no epitaphs on the Road of Graves. Existing doc covers identifying tattoo and bridge presence; this story adds full hierarchy, location, outcast mechanics, guardian bindings, and leadership.

- `[NEW]` `[EXPLICIT]` **Haruspex: divination via entrail-ghosts** -- A Haruspex in Deep Compost (named Joole) consults "ghosts of prophetic organs" for divination. A distinct diviner class reading entrail-ghosts rather than living anatomy. Price: three stamped squares of Lady Horsehair gold.
