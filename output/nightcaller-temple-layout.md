# Nightcaller Temple — Structural Layout

**Type:** Ruined clifftop fort, repurposed as a temple.
**Also known as:** Tower of Dawn, Syncope Sanctuary.
**Backstory:** Military fort → abandoned → occupied by cult → attacked by war party seeking to destroy a nightmare-inflicting artifact → cult released a sleep-gas (Miasma), sealing everyone inside. Ruin within a ruin.

---

## 1. Exterior
- Clifftop approach, guarded by three wild creatures.
- Berry bushes around the walls.
- **Climbable ruin:** scale wall right of entrance → rooftop → north side → hidden unlocked chest.
- **Ore vein** embedded in outer western wall.

## 2. Shrine Chamber (entry hall)
- Makeshift roadside-style shrine on left wall (set up by a lone outsider priest).
- Table under shrine holds a religious text.
- Lectern at back: three common books, novice-locked chest behind.
- Two vermin present pre-quest.
- **Sealed carving** at back wall: emits a mechanical hum. Blocks further passage until unlocked.

## 3. Inner Sanctum (upper tower ring)
- Carving opens into a shadowy curtain.
- **Central pit:** houses the artifact, encased in a magical barrier powered by a gem.
- **Overlook grating:** lets you view the artifact from above.
- Locked wooden door opposite grating (requires key).
- Stairs spiral down to barrier level; a raised wooden grate blocks a northwest passage.

## 4. Library
- Two floors, scattered cultists and invaders.
- Most books burnt.
- **Key item:** a specific tome explaining how to pass the barrier.
- Several surviving occult/daedric titles.
- Lower floor: wooden door hiding an expert-locked chest.

## 5. Storeroom → Laboratory
- Storeroom: food sacks, wine/mead shelves, alchemy ingredients, charcoal, leather scraps.
- **Laboratory:** three alchemy stations, heavy ingredient stock (wings, roots, salts, organs, ashes).
- **Key item:** a dream-inducing potion hidden among the alchemy stock.
- Skill book on alchemy, apothecary satchels, daedric text.
- Southeast wide passage exits via the previously blocked wooden grate (handle on left).

## 6. Dream-Barrier Crossing
- Drinking the potion forces a vision-walk re-enacting the Miasma release.
- Ends with retrieving the gem powering the barrier, collapsing it.

## 7. Passage to Sleeping Quarters
- Raised alcove: enchanter station, potions, soul gems, unlocked chest.
- Wardrobe alcove with clothes.
- Wine shelves.

## 8. Sleeping Quarters
- Dormitory: four beds, dressers, cult robes, unlocked chest.
- Lower dormitory: nine beds, mixed clothing, food sacks, adept-locked chest, weapon-and-coin table.
- **Dining hall branch:** long tables, heavy wine/mead stock, helmet, potions, soul gem fragments.

## 9. Inner Sanctum Finale
- Stair-room ascending to the artifact pedestal.
- Scripted confrontation with two surviving cultists.
- Unlocked boss chest beside pedestal.
- Shelves with mead and empty bottles.
