# Speech Card: The Child (sleepwalker) — working-context

**Positive mode:** Speaks only in numbered or itemized statements
**Register:** Formal-modern (rote liturgical cadence; the rite overrides her own register)
**Sentence-length cap:** 6 words maximum per unit of the rite
**Signature construction:** Ends every line on a noun

**Voice fingerprint:** Each clause of the funeral rite is a discrete item, short and closed; no connective tissue between lines; the child has no inflection of her own — she is a vessel for the rite's structure; lines end on nouns, flat and final.

**Default-banned:**
- Clinical or curious tone
- "What does it feel like" questions
- Detached observational commentary on the protagonist
- Fragment-as-style (the rite's itemized form is structural, not stylistic fragmentation)
