# Procession

This is the main body of the scene — the longest phase, where imagery, atmosphere, and slackened time become the content. Either *drift* (one image flowing associatively into the next) or *procession* (discrete tableaux in sequence — figures in a parade, rooms in a palace, dream-images down a corridor). The protagonist observes; no agency required. If the user provided **scene priorities**, keep these in mind as you write.

## Texture Budget

Draw on **most of the remaining textures** from your working document's inventory. Leave **1–2 textures** in reserve for the dissolution phase. This is where the bulk of your atmospheric material should land — woven into the drift or procession, not stacked on top of it.

## Guiding Questions

Use the following questions to shape your procession. You do not need to answer "yes" to every one — they are priorities to weigh, not a checklist to complete. Let the scene's needs dictate which matter most.

1. **Mode of progression**: Does the procession move by discrete tableaux, or by continuous associative drift?
2. **Forward motion**: Does the procession carry a sense of forward motion (physical or psychic), even without conventional plot causality?
3. **Specific particulars**: Are the procession's images rendered through specific, named particulars (objects, colors, textures) rather than generalized atmospherics ("eerie mist", "strange figures")?
4. **Loosening syntax**: Does the prose itself loosen — longer sentences, parataxis, comma-strings — to mirror the slackening of time?
5. **Witness stays passive**: Does the protagonist remain observer rather than slipping back into agent-mode?
6. **Forbidden interiority**: Does the procession resist deep interiority and analysis from the protagonist?
7. **Deepening detail**: Does the procession introduce at least one element stranger than what the threshold seeded, pushing the reverie deeper rather than holding a plateau?
8. **No explanatory pause**: Does the procession avoid pausing for explanation, summary, or contextualizing aside?
