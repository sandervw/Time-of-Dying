# Othelmedir's Black Scholastics: Story 1

**Overall Premise:** The story is part of a large selection of random ToD stories, based upon the **learnings and adventures of Othelmedir**, the necromancer.

## Outline - "The Dreamcatchers"

In the Road-village of *Sulky Wend*, near a sealed chrypt known as *The Tower of the Attercop*, residents live other lives at night, their dreams replaced by the death-visions of an old cult. Othelmedir knows the cause because he built it. Years ago, under a different name, he fled *The Tower* when a rival mancer cabal breached the walls and his own circle released a necromantic gas to buy time, sealing everyone inside as dreaming undead. Now he hires a sellsword, Weirmoth, returns to destroy the siphon before the villagers' memories are entirely replaced by those of the dreaming dead. Problem: the miasma preserved his old colleagues too, and they wake up as he enters.

Base plot on: https://en.uesp.net/wiki/Skyrim:Waking_Nightmare
