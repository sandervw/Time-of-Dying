# Waking Nightmare: Plot Outline

## Premise
A remote town suffers shared nightmares caused by a dormant artifact inside a nearby temple. A repentant priest recruits the protagonist to destroy it, concealing that he once served the very cult responsible.

## Act I: The Afflicted Town

### 1. Arrival
- Protagonist enters a town where every resident is sleep-deprived and short-tempered.
- The local ruler is unraveling; townsfolk beg for relief.

### 2. The Guide
- A wandering priest of a benevolent goddess waits at the inn.
- He identifies the cause: a dark deity stealing memories through dreams.
- He offers to lead the protagonist to the source, a ruined temple on a cliff.

## Act II: Into the Temple

### 3. The Sealed Door
- Outside the temple, the guide explains its history: long ago, invaders attacked the cult; the priests released a sleeping-gas ("Miasma") that put both sides into centuries-long slumber.
- He opens a magically hidden entryway.

### 4. First Obstacles
- Sleepers wake as the party advances: surviving invaders and cult devotees.
- The party reaches a balcony over an inner sanctum containing the artifact, but a magical barrier blocks access.

### 5. The Library
- The guide reveals he has a key: he was once a cult priest who fled the attack.
- Party fights through the library; retrieves a tome describing a dream-walking potion.

### 6. The Laboratory
- Party clears the alchemy wing and recovers the potion.
- Only the unaffiliated can safely drink it.

## Act III: The Dream

### 7. Dreamstride
- Protagonist drinks the potion and relives the original attack through the eyes of a past priest.
- Mistaken for that priest by cultists, the protagonist is instructed to release the Miasma.
- Protagonist pulls the release chain, dooming the original occupants to endless sleep.

### 8. Waking Through the Barrier
- Protagonist awakens on the far side of the barrier and disables it by removing a soul gem.

## Act IV: Confrontation

### 9. The Betrayal Revealed
- Approaching the artifact, the party meets two surviving cult priests, the guide's former brothers.
- They denounce him as a traitor who abandoned them. Combat follows.

### 10. The Ritual and the Choice
- The guide begins a ritual to destroy the artifact.
- The dark deity whispers to the protagonist, urging murder and theft.
- Final choice: let the guide destroy the artifact and gain an ally, or kill him and claim its power.

## Resolution
- Nightmares lift from the town regardless of choice.
