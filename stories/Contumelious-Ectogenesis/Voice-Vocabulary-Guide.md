# Voice Vocabulary Guide: Othelmedir

This document is a human-editable workbook that pairs with Necromancer-Voice.md. Fill in the sections below, then paste the relevant parts into each writing session alongside the voice template. The goal is to rotate vocabulary pressure so the LLM draws from different pools each time.

---

## How to Use This Document

1. Fill in each section below at your own pace.
2. Before a writing session, copy the **Session Prompt Block** at the bottom and fill in the blanks.
3. After a writing session, update the **Burned Vocabulary** list with overused terms from that session's output.
4. Rotate anchor passages and contextual buckets between sessions.

Do not use the following words or phrases in this scene. They have been overused in previous sessions:

When referencing [the necromancer-gods / the Road / necromantic concepts / other recurring entity], do not reuse established terms. Invent a new contextually appropriate epithet, folk title, or circumlocution each time.
