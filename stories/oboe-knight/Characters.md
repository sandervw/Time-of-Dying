# Story Character List

## Igon (Protagonist)

**Name:** Igon ("the Oboe Knight") of Ald Soad

**Summary:** A wind-worshipping pilgrim-knight. He descends into an ancestral tomb, drawn in search of a "beautiful, haunting, swan-like song of an oboe, which he heard on the wind as a child." He has obsessed over this song his whole life - finding it is his driving force, his reason for existence.

**Values:** Cooperation, faith, joy in struggle, self-reliance

**default personality:** Relentless optimism

**Weakness:** Fixation - cannot conceive of not finding the oboe

**Strength:** Passionate, strong
