# Two-Soul-Grave Story Ideas

## Overview

Basically: **"Assassins VS Vampires"**

A fanatical killer in the service of a religious assassins guild is slain while performing one of the guild's lawful Writs of Cutting Down; murdered before he can complete his task, by the clan of the vampire who was his target. His body is tossed out and sold to a local necromancer. The necromancer returns him to life (not un-dead - living again, but with a new heart since his old one was torn out).

The necromancer was a servant of *The Scratcher*. He attempted to replace the assassins soul with the soul of an ancient *Mailed Flayer*, a savage berserk warrior, creating a powerful thrall. What no one realized was that the Mailed Flayer's soul and the assassins are two incarnations of the same soul - a random twist of fate. The result was that the souls *merged*; two souls now live in the same body. Both souls were enraged at awakening from death, and slaughtered the necromancer.

Now the assassin is in a state of turmoil. He wants revenge; he wants to know why his religious assassins guild did not protect him or his body; devoted to his cause, he wants to complete his writ. Meanwhile he is at odds with the reincarnated half of his soul (the mailed flayer; the *Narrator*), who is equally devoted to death-dealing, but the mindless, frenzied, bloody, up-front kind of death which the silent, law-abiding assassin abhors.

The assassin must complete his writ, avenge his death on the one who orchestrated it, and resolve his new inner demon.
