## Essay

- **Lemma** - A single encyclopedia entry: monsters, locations, spells, diseases, items, whatever - always accompanied by an image (put in a PLACEHOLDER, possibly with short suggestion text for an image-model prompt); *replacement for TABLE*

## Lemma

Voice: Impersonal, authoritative, encyclopedic — no narrator presence, no "I" or "you," no emotional coloring. Hedges toward the scholarly with phrases like "is said to have" when attesting to legend vs. fact.
POV: Third-person omniscient/objective. The writer is an invisible cataloger who simply knows.
Tense: Mixed, governed by time-relation to the entity:

Present for enduring description / ongoing existence ("hilt is the shape of a serpent," "Typical weight exceeds two tons").
Past for origins and history ("was made by a nameless mancer," "drove the Road through her land").

One-liner: Terse, omniscient reference-prose that catalogs an entity's essential nature, origin, and place in the world as if transcribed from a worldly encyclopedia.

```
Example 1:
## The Old Soulwood
A named forest on the Road of Graves, predating the Time of Dying. A demon king willed his field to his human slave concubine on death. The necromancer-gods, unable to kill a human, drove the Road of Graves through her land instead. She planted magic juniper berries atop the first epitaphs; these rooted into stone and spread into an unstoppable forest. The Bouldsatr later settled it before being hunted to extinction.


Example 2:
## Dragon Eye Longsword
This fabulous weapon’s hilt is the shape of a serpent, with its fangs wrapped around a gleaming opal. It was made by a nameless mancer, who is said to have filled it with the hatred borne against mankind by the last, degenerated dragon. The Great Wyrm of The Leather Dragon is its bearer.

Example 3:
## Periwinkle Forest Cat
Sage Name: Felis animosilva major
Description: Among the largest living species of hunting cat: 4.3-4.5 meters in length, shoulder height of 2.2-2.3 meters, and a reach of about 3 meters. This cat species has unusually long and sinewy limbs, allowing it to spring and run with great agility, and even to climb trees. Its face is flat, with a less-powerful jaw comparative to its size. Typical weight exceeds two tons.
Habitat: The Soulwood and its quarters.
```
