# Aburaya — The Bathhouse of the Eight Million Gods

## Roof & Spires
- **Bell tower / cupola** — green-tiled peak crowning the structure
- **Rooftop** — where Haku (in dragon form) is often seen arriving or fleeing
- **Exterior shrines & offerings** — stone jizo, floral tribute, and the river spirit motif visible at the eaves

## Floor 6 — Yubaba's Penthouse
The opulent top floor, off-limits to staff except by summons.
- **Yubaba's office** — ornate Western-style sitting room, massive fireplace, the desk where Chihiro signs her contract
- **Boh's nursery** — oversized pillows and toys for the giant baby
- **Private corridors** — gilded, claustrophobic, lined with doors

## Floor 5 — Foreman & Senior Staff
- **Aniyaku / Chichiyaku quarters** — the frog-man and slug-woman supervisors
- **Account rooms** — where the day's tokens and tabs are tallied

## Floor 4 — The Grand Bridge & Main Hall
The iconic public face of the bathhouse.
- **Red arched bridge** — the threshold gods cross on arrival (where No-Face first lingers)
- **Main entryway hall** — bowing attendants greet incoming spirits
- **Tatami reception rooms** — for high-status guests

## Floor 3 — Worker Dormitories & Linen
- **Yuna sleeping quarters** — shared tatami rooms (Lin and Chihiro bunk here)
- **Linen and uniform storage**
- **Staff stair landings** connecting up to the baths

## Floor 2 — The Bath Halls
The working heart of the operation.
- **Public soaking tubs** — segmented stalls for various spirits
- **The Big Tub** — the oversized deluxe bath used for the Stink Spirit / River Spirit cleansing
- **Herbal soak token chutes** — where bath tokens drop down from Kamaji's boiler

## Floor 1 / Sub-level — The Boiler Room
Kamaji's domain — the bathhouse's literal engine.
- **Kamaji's workstation** — the many-armed spider-yokai surrounded by herb drawers
- **Susuwatari (soot sprite) zone** — coal-hauling, star-candy feeding station
- **Floor hatch** — leads up to the bath chutes
- **Side door** — the "back way" Chihiro uses; opens onto the exterior stairs

## Exterior / Below
- **Long wooden staircase** — the rickety descent down the cliffside
- **Pig pens** — somewhere out back, where Chihiro's parents are kept
- **Train platform** — across the flooded plain, accessed past the boiler room exit
