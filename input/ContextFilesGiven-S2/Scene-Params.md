# Scene Parameters: dreamreel-s2

**Senses:** Touch / Proprioception / Sight
Touch foregrounds the physical unease of sleep-starved bodies and the tactile detail of Weirmoth's soap and Othelmedir's cloak; proprioception carries the power dynamics of men sizing each other up across a counter; sight locks the visual inventory (prosthetic arm, shroud-cloak, spear) without over-saturating with colour.

**Rhythms:** Run-on / Cumulative
Dialogue spills and accumulates clause on clause, mirroring the bedmeister's exhausted over-explanation and Othelmedir's formal accretion.

**Scene Shape:** Deflation
The scene opens with desperate pleading and ends in a flat, practical agreement; no triumph, only resignation.

**Colour Palette:** Monochromatic-grey
Sulky Wend's sleep-starved pallor; ash and shadow dominate, with oxblood as the only punctuation.
