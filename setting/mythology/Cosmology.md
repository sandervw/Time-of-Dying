# Cosmology: The Road of Graves

## Overview

The world is the Road of Graves -- an endless road paved with the gravestones of every person who has ever died. It runs in one main course without ends; over mountains and under rivers; through forests, caves, and castles; sometimes curving, rarely straight. The Road is ever-expanding. When any man dies, his gravestone is added to the cobbles of the Road.

The sun is cold and slate-colored.

### The Cursed Forest

The Road is surrounded by the Cursed Forest -- a wasteland of trees, poison pools, and roaming undead. Trees sprout from the ground rotting and skeletal. Leaves don't come from the trees; they arrive born on the wind of some faraway plane of forever-autumn, already fallen and colored with decay.

### Fauna

The only birds are ravens, crows, whip-poor-wills, and vultures.

### Beneath the Road

Some graves -- Tombs and Chrypts -- are more elaborate than simple stones. They are deep underground vaults. Chryptsackers are professionals in the art of looting these valuable troves.

## Landmarks and Notable Places

### The Lake of Tomatoes

- Reached via the Road of Skins
- A many-chambered pit of slightly acidic, pinkish-translucent bile, with floating round chunks
- The deepest part of the Road, which devours all things that fall into it
- Gallbladder is the chief god of this place; worshipped by mutant, amalgamated, tribal farmers
- Lots of long, segmented worms stuck in place in the lake

### Middlemoss

- The main and starting village/tomb
- Lies in "The Middle of the Road"; also in the middle of a cliff along which the Road runs
- Paper lanterns buzzing faintly with glowbugs inside
- Massive fungal conservatory -- many mushroom bonsai

### Thistlecrown

- A motte and bailey where the Road runs over the tallest mountain
- Half-frozen cabins
- Lies in the shadow of a high, frozen cathedral to The Old Man Below

### Other Places

- Scratcher's Temple
- Tombtown
- The Living, Breathing, Mossy Gulch
- The Rat Farmer's Barn
- Embalming-fluid pipeline from one refinery to outlying necromancer facilities

## Subterranean Geography

### The Oldvaults

- Once the location of the necromancers' dungeons and laboratories; now a place of forgotten vaults, with doors often locked from the outside
- All exits to the Cursed Forest are sealed, or said to be
- Undead are most common here, as are weird experiments and relics of ancient times

### The Sleep Tombs

- Mancers came down here and entered eternal slumber, to be awakened again in a better age, with a new sun dawned
- They set a monster to guard them

### Other Subterranean Places

- The Crypt of Unconsecrated Ashes (where sinners were burned, their ashes strewn)
- The Abbottoir of the Carnivores
