# Cosmology: The Road of Graves

## Overview

The world is the Road of Graves -- an endless road paved with the gravestones of every person who has ever died. It runs in one main course without ends; over mountains and under rivers; through forests, caves, and castles; sometimes curving, rarely straight. The Road is ever-expanding. When any man dies, his gravestone is added to the cobbles of the Road.

The sun is cold and slate-colored.

The Road is surrounded by a wasteland of trees, poison pools, and roaming undead. Trees sprout from the ground rotting and skeletal. Leaves don't come from the trees; they arrive born on the wind of some faraway plane of forever-autumn, already fallen and colored with decay.

The only birds are ravens, crows, whip-poor-wills, and vultures.

Some graves - Tombs and Chrypts - are more elaborate than simple stones. They are deep underground vaults. Chryptsackers are professionals in the art of looting these valuable troves.

## Landmarks

### The Lake of Tomatoes

- Reached via the Road of Skins
- A many-chambered pit of slightly acidic, pinkish-translucent bile, with floating round chunks
- The deepest part of the Road, which devours all things that fall into it
- Gallbladder is the chief god of this place; worshipped by mutant, amalgamated, tribal farmers
- Lots of long, segmented worms stuck in place in the lake
