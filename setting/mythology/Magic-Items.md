# Magic Items and Sundries

## Overview

Necromantic tools, substances, reagents, and curios. Some are crafted deliberately; others grow in the cursed landscape or are byproducts of spellwork. A working mancer's field kit typically includes flints, a shovel, a steel basinet, and whatever reagents the job demands.

## Substances

- **Violet Jelly** -- Unguent which melts tissue, but not bones.
- **Embalming Fluid** -- Transported via pipeline from refineries to outlying necromancer facilities.
- **Banshee-wax** -- Purpose unspecified; a common field-kit component.
- **Bone Dust** -- Carried in flour sacks; a common necromantic reagent.
- **Fabricated Tissue** -- Artificially created biological material, white and marble-like. Can be worn as armor or used as a component. Suggests mancers can grow living-adjacent tissue.

## Tools

- **Firebug Lantern** -- A lantern containing firebugs; standard mancer field equipment.

## Curios

### Singing Mushroom

- A fungus with a tiny human mouth on the underside of its cap
- Capable of humming songs; used as a necromantic tool
- Its humming appears connected to incomplete Deadspeech spellwork
