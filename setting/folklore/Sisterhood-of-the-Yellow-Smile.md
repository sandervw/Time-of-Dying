# The Sisterhood of the Yellow Smile

## Overview

A religious order that practices living burial as deliberate soul-vouchsafing. The Sisterhood maintains priories defended by glass illusions and operates under a strict oath-and-interment framework: sworn oaths are cosmologically linked to post-death protection, and breaking an oath forfeits that shield.

## Living Burial

The Sisterhood buries the living as a deliberate act of soul protection. Interment is a choice available during life, not only a posthumous consequence. Once a living person's burial is pledged to a Sisterhood stone, the placement is incontrovertible - a loophole must be found; the pledge cannot simply be revoked.

## Oath-Breaking and Afterlife Forfeiture

A soul that breaks sworn oaths voluntarily surrenders interment's shield and accepts mancer capture as just consequence. Sworn oaths are structurally linked to post-death protection within the Sisterhood's framework.

## Glass Illusions (Priory Defenders)

Four typed glass defenders guard Sisterhood priories. Distinct from the False Foe (a static fear-statue) and from glass golems.

- **Empty Robe Man** - Fragile-legged; avoid eye contact.
- **Crying Child** - Advances only when not watched.
- **Body Faker** - Must be shattered exactly thirteen times; reassembles each time.
- **Veilface** - Moves as jagged fog; slow and cutting; use shield.
