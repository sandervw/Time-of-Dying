# Monks of the Lidless

## Overview

A monastic order identifiable by a shaved scalp and a tattooed small mouth inside a black ink circle. Not linked to any of the seven necromancer-gods. The order's name references the Lidless -- malevolent winged half-demons that haunt churches, towers, and barns (see `Mythology/Creatures.md`). The order has no epitaphs on the Road of Graves. Their seat is the Tower of Silence in Deep Compost.

## Hierarchy and Leadership

- **The Senseless King and Queen** -- Unliving crowned figures who lead the order. Their skulls are covered in owl, goat, snake, and spider eye tattoos. They wield iron-hook scepters
- Three named fighter types serve the order: **Tuskhands**, **Man of Dogs**, and **Leaping Monk**

## The Tower of Silence

The order's seat in Deep Compost. Will-o-Wisps are bound as guards within the tower. A Starshadow serves as the tower's active guardian-warden, enforcing internal rules (e.g., cursing those who take more than one statue).

## Outcasts: The Wine Chrypt

Blind monks who fall from the order's favor are exiled to The Wine Chrypt. Outcasts are wrapped in owl-feather blankets and fed on soured ancient wine.

## Sacred Reliquary

The order maintains a sacred reliquary containing named relics:

- **The Quieting Mirror** -- Quiets one's thinking; sought as a cure for night terrors (see `Mythology/Magic-Items.md`)
- **Scroll of Centipedes** -- Named scroll relic; function unstated

## Known Presence

- Present on Middlemoss chasm bridges
- Hostile toward Lady Horsehair's supplicants
